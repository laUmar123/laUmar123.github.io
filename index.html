The Battle of RPS Land

The Lore

The year was 6 BC, the town of RPS had be ravaged with plagues and crime. With such an evil filled town, the knights had decided it would be time to destroy the town once and for all. News broke out to the people of RPS and they all gathered to choose a leader to defend this town. With no one willing to step forward, all hope was lost, but there emerged a man; face covered with a red cloth, wearing a shiny white dress. He offered to fight this knight, but made everyone vow to stop the crime in this land, and work with one another. With no other option, the people of RBS accepted his offer, and asked how he would defeat this unformidable knight. He put down a rock, a shield and two swords, and said "no more questions".

The Rules

  1. You have 3 items: a powerful rock, shield and swords, you must choose between these
  2. The knight will also have access to the same weapons and will choose one of them per round
  3. The powerful rock will beat the sword but be blocked by the shield
  4. The shield will block the powerful rock but swords would destroy the shield
  5. The swords will break the shield but will not be able to block the powerful rock
  6. You and the knight both have 5 lives, the first person to go to 0 lives loses and thus dies
  7. Enjoy! May fortune favour you against this formidable knight.

You Have Defeated the Knight!

The town cheers and everyone circles around you. Everyone is cheering your name and they are all dancing in happiness, your work has been completed and you have saved this town. They offer to make you their king, and swear to never cause trouble again. You collect your weapons that have been damaged during battle, but a small smirk appears on your face knowing you can't be defeated.

The Hero Has Been Slain!

Sorrow runs over each person, their fate has been sealed and they scream in fear, the knight picks his sword that has been impaled into your body, and lets out a loud demonic laughter. Everyone flees but they all know they have no where to run, the knight takes the final steps in this future non-existant town.
